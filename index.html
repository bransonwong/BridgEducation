About BridgeEd

BridgeEd was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality education accessible and affordable for all students while providing valuable earning opportunities for college students with academic expertise.

Our platform bridges this gap by connecting students with top-performing college students who have excelled in their respective subjects.

Through our innovative online platform, we've eliminated geographical barriers, allowing students to connect with qualified tutors from anywhere in the world.
